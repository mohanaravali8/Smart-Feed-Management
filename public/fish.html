🐟 Fish & 🦐 Shrimp Life Cycles

🐟 Fish Life Cycle

  • 🥚 **Eggs** – Laid in water, protected by adults.
  • 🔬 **Embryo** – Development begins inside the egg.
  • 🐣 **Larva** – Small & transparent, starts feeding.
  • 🐟 **Juvenile Fish** – Grows scales & shape develops.
  • 🦈 **Adult Fish** – Fully grown, capable of reproduction.

🦐 Shrimp Life Cycle

  • ❤️ **Mating** – Adult male & female breed.
  • 🥚 **Ovigerous (Egg Carrying)** – Female carries eggs.
  • 👶 **Shrimplets (25-35 Days)** – Tiny baby shrimps hatch.
  • 🐾 **Juvenile (60 Days)** – Grow bigger & stronger.
  • 🦐 **Adult (15 Days)** – Fully grown, can reproduce again.
