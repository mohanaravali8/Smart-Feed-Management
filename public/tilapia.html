Tilapia Fish

Scientific Name

Oreochromis niloticus

Category

Freshwater Fish

Origin

Africa

Average Weight

1 - 5 kg

Food Habits

Tilapia feeds on algae, aquatic plants, and plankton.

Water Temperature

25°C - 30°C

Importance

Tilapia is one of the most farmed fish species worldwide due to rapid growth and disease resistance.
