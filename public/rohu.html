Rohu Fish

Scientific Name

Labeo rohita

Category

Freshwater Fish

Origin

India, Bangladesh, Nepal, Pakistan

Average Weight

2 - 10 kg

Food Habits

Rohu mainly feeds on plants, algae, and organic matter.

Water Temperature

22°C - 32°C

Importance

Rohu is one of the most popular aquaculture fish species in India. It grows quickly and has high market demand.
