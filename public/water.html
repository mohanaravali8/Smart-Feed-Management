💧 Monthly Water Change Guide

Follow this schedule to maintain water quality and fish health.

MonthWater Change
January - FebruaryChange 25% every 15 days
March - May (Summer)Change 30–40% every 10 days
June - September (Rainy)Change 15–20% every 20 days
October - DecemberChange 20% every 15 days
